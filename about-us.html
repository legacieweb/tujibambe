About Tujibambe

Discover the story behind Kenya's premier adventure company

Our Story

Tujibambe was born from a passion for Kenya's untamed wilderness and a vision to share its magic with adventurers from around the world. Founded in 2005 by a team of Kenyan explorers and conservationists, we've been crafting unforgettable experiences that blend heart-pounding adventure with deep cultural immersion.

Our name "Tujibambe" comes from the Swahili word meaning "let's go explore" - a philosophy that drives everything we do. We believe that true adventure isn't just about thrill-seeking; it's about connecting with nature, understanding local cultures, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional adventure experiences that inspire conservation, support local communities, and create lifelong memories while maintaining the highest standards of safety and sustainability.
